Permissions & Privacy

IronFox only requests permissions that are used to support features you choose to use.

📷 Camera Access

Used to take progress photos for comparisons and tracking. Photos can be stored inside the app or optionally saved to your device photo library.

🖼️ Photo Library Access

Used to import existing photos for progress comparisons and optionally save photos created within IronFox.

❤️ Health Data Access

Used to read HealthKit information including step count, distance traveled, flights climbed, and calories to help track activity progress. IronFox only accesses data you explicitly allow.

🔔 Notifications

Used for workout reminders, milestone notifications, goal reminders, and scheduled workout alerts. Notifications can be changed or disabled anytime.
