Hello my name is Cameron Fox, and I am a Computer Science student at the University of New Mexico with a passion for building software that blends creativity, usability, and clean engineering. Born and raised in Albuquerque, I’ve always been drawn to technology, whether it was photography, video games, or taking apart electronics just to see how they worked.

I love creating projects with a visual, interactive component, especially ones that feel polished and intuitive to anyone who uses them. I enjoy making ideas come alive through software.

Outside of coding, I’m happiest spending time with the people I care about, whether it’s a night in, exploring new restaurants, or heading out on an adventure. I love spending time outside: backpacking, hiking, snowboarding, and enjoying the beauty of New Mexico’s mountains. I’m also a huge soccer fan, specifically going to New Mexico United games. When I want something more laid-back, you’ll find me enjoying photography, movies, puzzles, or video games.

My goal is to build software that feels thoughtful and creative—tools that help people and bring ideas to life. I approach every project with curiosity and determination, always looking for ways to grow and refine my craft. Ultimately, I want to create software that sparks creativity, solves real problems, and leaves a positive mark on the people who use it.
